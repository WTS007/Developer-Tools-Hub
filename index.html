Client-Side Tools

A collection of useful utilities that run entirely in your browser. Your data stays on your machine.

JPEG Compressor

Optimize JPEG images by adjusting the compression quality.

Universal Image Converter

Convert images to JPEG or PDF with quality adjustments.

Image Editor

Crop, rotate, and flip your images before exporting.

PDF Compressor

Reduce the size of PDFs by compressing the images inside.

Folder Compressor

Create a ZIP file from a folder, with image optimization.
